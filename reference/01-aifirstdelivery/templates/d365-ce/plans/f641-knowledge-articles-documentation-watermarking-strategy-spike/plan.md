### Module Architecture Summary

Configuration-only feature: KB Article governance via versioning, audit, and language-aware Knowledge Search (per Constitution §15 + Blueprint slides 43-46 — KB migration from SHELF, KB video / attachment patterns). No SHELF migration in scope here — that is a separate Data Migration feature.

**Data Flow:**
```
Author → Knowledge Article (parent) → translated children (one per language) → Knowledge Search → user-language article
```

> **AI Notes** — Architecture decision: **configuration-first** (per Constitution §00). No new integrations introduced — feature relies on existing Fusion → D365 sync (Blueprint slides 50–51) and surfaces results via D365 forms / views. Multi-language posture from day one (Constitution §15) means RESX-based string loading even though only `en-US` is initially shipped.
